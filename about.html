ABOUT

Deltares in an independent knowledge institute for applied research in the field of water and subsoil. Deltares works worldwide on smart innovations, solutions and applications for people, the environment and society.

Deltares mainly focuses on deltas, coastal regions, and river areas. Because the management of these densely populated and vulnerable areas is complex, Deltares works closely with governments, companies, knowledge institutes and universities around deltas, coastal regions, and river areas or at home in The Netherlands.

Their motto is 'Enabling Delta Life'. As an applied knowledge institute, Deltares is successful if its knowledge is redeemed in and for society. Deltares sets high standards for the quality of its knowledge and advice.

All assignments and projects, whether financed by the market or from funds for strategic research, contribute to strengthening the knowledge base. In addition, Deltares attaches great importance to openness and transparency. This attitude is reflected, among other things, in making the software and models developed by Deltares freely accessible. Open source works is their firm conviction. Deltares employs 877 people, 39 different nationalities and is based in Delft and Utrecht.
